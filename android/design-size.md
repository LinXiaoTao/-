## Android 启动页尺寸

### 格式

**9-Patch PNG**

### 尺寸

* LDPI
  * Portrait: **200x320px**
  * Landscape: **320x200px**
* MDPI
  * Portrait: **320x480px**
  * Landscape: **480x320px**
* HDPI
  * Portrait: **480x800px**
  * Landscape: **800x480px**
* XHDPI
  * Portrait: **780x1280px**
  * Landscape: **1280x720px**
* XXDPI
  * Portrait: **960x1600px**
  * Landscape: **1600x960px**
* XXXDPI
  * Portrait: **1280x1920px**
  * Landscape: **1920x1280px**
